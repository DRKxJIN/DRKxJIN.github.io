What is Affiliate?

Affiliate marketing is a performance-driven channel that enables a rich ecosystem of partnerships to reach, influence, and convert shoppers worldwide at every part of their purchase journey, both online and offline. Affiliate is the place where partnerships and technology converge to create valuable shopping experiences that result in scalable, profitable growth..

Where do you fit in?

Publisher Partners
Publisher partners engage shoppers through a variety of distribution models including websites, social media, blogs, search, email, mobile apps, offline marketing, and more.
Advertiser Partners
Advertisers are brands that sell products, experiences, and services and reward publishers for driving desired results such as sales, leads, new customer acquisition, influence in the path-to-purchase, high-value shoppers, or anything else you can dream up.
